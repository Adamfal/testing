Open Positions

Foreign Rights Agent:

We are looking for a Foreign Rights Agent to join our wonderful team. We are Aesthetic Press, a Full-Service independent, diverse, commercial Digital-First publishing company. As a Full-Service company, we are interested in being able to offer our authors representation internationally. It is our goal to support diversity and inclusion within the publishing industry. Our ideal candidate is looking to represent Adult, Young Adult, and New Adult Thrillers, Mysteries, Dark Fiction and books with Dark Humor and Anti-Heroes. All with commercial and upmarket appeal.

Foreign Rights Agent Responsibilities:

Arranging meetings with editors at publishing houses internationally to represent book proposals, confirm meetings and follow through with publishers once meeting are fulfilled. Communicate in a professional and considerate manner representing diverse authors. Negotiating and Tracking author contracts. Attending Conferences and Events within the publishing industry (This is flexible in the COVID-19 era and can be accomplished virtually)

Commission Pay Only (Above average at 30%)

Qualifications:
  • Sales Record in Publishing
  • Communication and writing skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Training in Literary Agenting (Completed Job Experience, Internship, and/or Mentorship)
  • Advanced knowledge of the commercial and upmarket fiction trends, marketplace, and passion for the genres we publish here at Aesthetic Press
  • English speaking (bi-lingual is a plus, but not required
  • Editorial Experience (a plus, but not required)

Location: REMOTE

How to Apply:
Send Cover Letter, Resumé, and a list of five (5) favorite dark fiction books with 3-4 sentences for each about why you like them and how you would market them to an editor. Send material to AestheticPressBooks@gmail.com with the Job Title in the Subject Line.
