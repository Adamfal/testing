Who We Are & What We Are Looking For!

Aesthetic Press is a Full-Service Digital-First Independent Publisher of commercial fiction Novels, Novelettes, & Anthologies. We specialize in Thrillers, Mysteries, Dark Fiction, and books with Dark Humor, Anti-Heroes, & Compelling/Gritty Themes! Aesthetic Press has a commitment to supporting diversity and opening doors for creatives in publishing! In the field of publishing black and brown authors are underrepresented and underutilized. There are few fields, avenues, and companies which seek literature from BIPOC authors and diverse literature and if they do it is rarely consistent, but Aesthetic Press is an indie publisher that welcomes diverse work and offers extensive marketing for our titles! We seek to serve, nurture, cater to a long forgotten, abandoned, underrepresented market of imaginative, diverse artists. 81% of all authors published in America are white. 79% of all people who work in the publishing industry are white. “Publishing So White” is a real thing and is a real concern for the hundreds of thousands of underrepresented authors with stories to tell that have commercial potential to make their voices heard and get their art seen. We are particularly interested in compelling storylines/characters, anti-heroes, stories that reflect reality/current events/social justice, reality bending tales, stories with series potential, single author anthologies…all genres and themes are welcome and will be considered, but the listed ones are our absolute favorites! Aesthetic Press welcomes unsolicited submissions from unagented & agented authors.

We accept the following genres:

  • Fiction (fiction only) in the form of novels
  • Novelettes, and novellas.
  • We accept Adult, Young Adult, and New Adult. .
  • Thrillers and psychological thrillers.
  • Dark humor.
  • Mysteries.
  • Murder mysteries.
  • Super villains.
  • Dystopian.
  • Quirky and Academia.
  • Forbidden/Dark romance.
  • Historical/period.
  • Anthologies

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

As stated above, we only publish fiction books according to our requested genres. If the genre of your book is not within our list you can still submit as we are open to all genres aswell. We try to respond with personal responses to all submissions that follow the guidelines within an estimate of 6-8 weeks. “Stephen King was turned down 80 times by publishers, with his horror story Carrie. Now, the novel has been translated into hundreds of languages, and has been adapted both into a play and a musical.” From submission to release, we want to guide you and your book every step of the way to the greatest success! We will be publishing six (6) standout books during our first year in operation during this time expect to have the freedom to engage in a back-and-forth discussion over the edits done to your manuscript and your customized cover design! We will publish simultaneous Paperback and E-Book editions of your book to Amazon, Kindle Unlimited, Apple Books, Google Books, and KOBO. We will market your book through extensive paid promotion and advertisement, campaigns on Instagram and Facebook, virtual Cover Reveal events, a wide-spread virtual book/author book tour spanning more than a dozen well-known book blogs and BookTube hosts! We will also advocate for the books we publish to film producers, film directors, studios, networks, and theaters around the world to try and sell the movie/adaptation rights to your story to get it adapted into a movie, television, or stage play production! We offer generous royalties for our authors at 50%! We do not pay advances yet!

HOW TO SUBMIT

What to include in your QUERY Submission: Query Letter w/Manuscript word count, Genre, and Comparative work and at least two other books that are similar to your manuscript. (We do not open attachments – any submissions with attachments will not be considered and automatically rejected & deleted). Paste the first Three (3) Chapters (or first 50 pages) of your manuscript in the body of the email. A synopsis of (max 1000 words) with spoilers and plot included! In your submission include your name, email address, and contact info and a summary of previous publications/awards, recognition for your work by institutions or other parties. Send your submission to: AestheticPressBooks@gmail.com With the following subject: “QUERY – PROJECT TITLE – YOUR NAME” *We try to respond with personal responses to all submissions (that follow the guidelines) within six-eight weeks.
